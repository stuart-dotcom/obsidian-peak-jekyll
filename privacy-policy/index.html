1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide, including:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number, and business name when you fill out a contact form or inquiry.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit our Site, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage data: Pages visited, time spent, referring URLs, and browser/device type.
  • IP address: Used for security, analytics, and approximate geographic location.
  • Cookies: See Section 5 for details on our cookie use.

If you are a client of ours, we will process data about your customers on your behalf through the Peak Platform (e.g., CRM contact data, SMS conversations). In this context, you are the data controller and we act as a data processor. We process such data only as directed by you and in accordance with our service agreement.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and communicate with you about our Services.
  • Provide, set up, and manage your marketing system and services.
  • Process payments and maintain billing records.
  • Send service updates, onboarding instructions, and support communications.
  • Improve our Site, services, and processes.
  • Comply with legal obligations.
  • Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your information for behavioral advertising or share it with data brokers.

Where required by applicable law (such as GDPR), we process your personal data based on:
