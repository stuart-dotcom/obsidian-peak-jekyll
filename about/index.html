About Obsidian Peak

Built by a Business Owner, for Business Owners.

Obsidian Peak Holdings was founded in Lake Placid, Florida with one goal: give local businesses access to the same marketing power that big brands use — without the agency markup, the long contracts, or the learning curve.

We're not a marketing conglomerate or a faceless software company. We're a small, focused operation that goes deep with each client — learning your business, your market, and what actually moves the needle for you.

Our Mission

Level the Playing Field

Large corporations spend millions on CRM platforms, marketing automation, and ad tech. For years, local businesses had no access to any of it. We built the Peak Platform to change that — and we manage it for businesses who don't have the time or technical knowledge to run it themselves.

How We Work

Our Core Principles

01

Results Over Vanity

We don't care about impressions, reach, or engagement if it doesn't translate to more customers and more revenue for you. Every metric we track ties back to your bottom line.

02

Done For You

You have a business to run. We don't hand you a login and a YouTube tutorial. We set everything up, explain what it does, and keep it running. You focus on your craft — we handle the marketing.

03

Straight Talk

No jargon. No over-promising. If something isn't working, we'll tell you. We'd rather set honest expectations and overdeliver than win a sale and disappoint.

04

Long-Term Thinking

We're not interested in one-time wins. Every system we build is designed to compound — getting more effective over time as your reputation grows and your pipeline fills.
